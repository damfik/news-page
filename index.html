Prince Carl Philip, Duke of Värmland

On Friday, the proud parents returned home to Villa Solbacken, posing for a photo with their new bundle of joy at the hospital before leaving. Sofia looked incredible in the photo, showing off an impressive post-baby body merely one day after giving birth. The royal wore a white top, grey jacket and casual jeans. Last summer, Sofia spoke about the joys of motherhood, telling Sweden's Svenskdam: "It's amazing. Having children changes your whole life." She also noted, "[Alexander] is very sweet and well-behaved." Carl Philip also discussed his new role as a father, telling the outlet that it took "some adjustment, of course, but it is still absolutely amazing and wonderful".
